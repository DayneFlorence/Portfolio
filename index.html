About

Dayne Florence is an Australian performing artist and web developer from Sydney.

After receiving his education at the Mcdonald College of Performing Arts and Ballettschule Theater Basel, Dayne began his career with the Ballet du Rhin in Mulhouse, France, as an apprentice. He then became a member of the NRW Junior Ballet Dortmund in Germany, a soloist with Nürnberg Ballet in Germany, and is currently residing in Basel, Switzerland, as a member of Ballet Basel. During his career, Dayne has performed works by Hofesh Shechter, Marco Goecke, Alexander Ekman, Richard Wherlock, Johan Inger, Rachelle Scott, Jacopo Godani, Craig Davidson, Goyo Montero, Xin Peng Wang, amongst others.

Besides his work as a performer, Dayne is a self-taught web developer. With a passion for using his creativity to build ideas from scratch, he enjoys working with people to help create personal and professional websites and web applications.
